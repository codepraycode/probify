## 🧮 1.3 – Calculating Simple Probabilities

Now that you know <BoldText>what</BoldText> probability is, let's learn <BoldText>how to calculate</BoldText> it!


<Note type="formula">
The Probability Formula:
$$
P(E) = \frac{\text{Favourable Outcomes}}{\text{Possible Outcomes}}
$$
</Note>

  <Example title="🎯 Dice Roll">
    Chance of rolling a 3:
    <Math>P(3) = \frac{1}{6} ≈ 0.1667</Math>
  </Example>
  
  <Example title="🃏 Card Draw">
    Chance of drawing a ♥ from a deck:
    <Math>P(Heart) = \frac{13}{52} = \frac{1}{4}</Math>
  </Example>



<List ordered title="📝 Probability Calculation Steps">
  <ListItem>🔍 Identify all possible outcomes (Sample Space)</ListItem>
  <ListItem>🧮 Count your favourable outcomes</ListItem>
  <ListItem>➗ Divide favourable by possible</ListItem>
  <ListItem>✨ Simplify the fraction (if needed)</ListItem>
  <ListItem>🔄 Convert to decimal/percentage (optional)</ListItem>
</List>
